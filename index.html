In the year following Nicholas's graduation, Mr. Matuanga approached him with a unique proposition.
Recognizing Nicholas's leadership qualities and academic prowess, he offered him the opportunity to assist the teachers in teaching.

Nicholas accepted the offer with gratitude and humility, eager to contribute to the educational environment that had shaped him.

His mother made the difficult decision to divorce his father, leaving Nicholas and his father to face new challenges.

During this time, Nicholas's grandmother stepped in to lend a helping hand.
